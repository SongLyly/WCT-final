Who We Are???

Founded in 2016, LeeSong Collection is a fast-fashion apparel and accessories brand retailer based in Cambodia that exists for the love and like of fashion, our clothes are carefully selected to offer quality products at affordable prices. As a Phnom Penh based company with 2 retail stores throughout Cambodia, we sell collections for women and men. We make fashion both unique and affordable, so everyone can get his or her wardrobe filled with the latest trendy outfits and be confidence to be whoever they want to be.

LeeSong is able to stay on top of the latest fashion trends from around the globe while rapidly bringing these styles to Cambodia market

We believe everyone can stay fashionable in style without breaking the bank.
